README




We compiled four annual condition variables for tree cover ET- two abiotic condition variables (rainfall and temperature), and two landscape condition variables (NDVI, and area burnt). 


Reference conditions were calculated by moku tree cover ET. For rainfall and temperature they are the mean rainfall and mean temperatures respectively aggregated over 1990-1992, for NDVI reference conditions they are the mean NDVI over 2001-2003, and for burnt area reference conditions they are the mean burnt area over 1999-2001. All four reference conditions are chosen based on earliest available data, and a 3-year aggregate was used to prevent bias due to an unusual data year.


Source: Rainfall, temperature, NDVI (Hawaiʻi Climate Data Portal); Area Burnt (Hawaiʻi Wildfire Management Organization)
